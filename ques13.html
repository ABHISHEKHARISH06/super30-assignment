Welcome to the Positioning Dance Floor!

Each box demonstrates a different CSS positioning type with a dance analogy.

Static Positioning

Default position, stays in normal document flow.

"The Shy Dancer" - Stays with the group, doesn't stand out.

Relative Positioning

Positioned relative to its normal position.

Using: top: 20px; left: 30px;

"The Two-Step" - Moves from original position but leaves space.

Absolute Positioning

Positioned relative to nearest positioned ancestor.

Using: bottom: 50px; right: 50px;

"The Solo Performer" - Breaks from group, does own thing.

Sticky Positioning

Toggles between relative and fixed based on scroll.

Using: top: 100px;

"The Limbo Dancer" - Sticks when you scroll past.

Fixed Positioning

Positioned relative to viewport.

"The Moonwalker" - Always in the same spot.
